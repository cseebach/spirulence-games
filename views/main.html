Windmaster in 30 Seconds:

You have 10 minutes to build 4 Dome Generators.

Controls:

Left click
on one of the building types on the bottom of the screen, and you'll be able to place it if you've produced one or more of it.

Right click
on one of the building types, and you will queue it's production.

Click
on a building in the queue, and it will be removed.

Click on "Goal:"
to see technologies available for research.
Click "CHOOSE"
to start researching one. Some buildings will not be enabled until you research a certain technology.

Press SPACE
at any time to pause the game.
